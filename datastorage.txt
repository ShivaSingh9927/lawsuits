Recommended Storage Solutions
Since you are already on Hetzner, you have two primary professional paths:

Hetzner Object Storage (S3 Compatible)
Hetzner offers S3-compatible storage. This is the most cost-effective and reliable method. It ensures your images are decoupled from your VPS, so if your server crashes, your images remain safe.

The Media Processing Pipeline
To keep your Vercel frontend fast, you shouldn't just "upload and forget." You need a pipeline to optimize these luxury images.

The Workflow:
Upload: Your Admin Dashboard (Next.js) sends the image to your Hetzner backend.

Processing: Your backend uses a library like Sharp (Node.js) to:

Convert to WebP: Significantly smaller file size with better quality.

Generate Multiple Sizes: Create a thumbnail, medium, and large (hero) version.

Apply Watermarks: (Optional) To protect your bespoke designs.

Storage: The backend pushes these versions to Object Storage.

Database Entry: You save the paths in your product_images table:

JSON
{
  "original": "https://storage.yourbrand.com/suits/navy-suit-full.webp",
  "thumbnail": "https://storage.yourbrand.com/suits/navy-suit-thumb.webp"
}
4. Content Delivery Network (CDN)
Even if your files are on Hetzner in Germany, a lawyer in Mumbai or New York needs to see them instantly.

Cloudflare: Point your storage domain to Cloudflare. It will cache your images in "edge" locations globally.

Vercel Image Optimization: Since your frontend is on Vercel, you can use their <Image /> component. It automatically serves the right size and format based on the user's device.

5. Handling Sensitive Data (Measurements)
Since you are storing Home Measurement photos or profiles, security is paramount.

Private Buckets: Keep the "Product" bucket public, but the "Customer Measurements" bucket private.

Signed URLs: When a tailor needs to see a customer’s measurement photo, your backend generates a Signed URL that expires in 15 minutes. This ensures the data isn't just sitting on the open internet.

6. Folder Structure for Organization
Keep your storage bucket organized from Day 1:

/products/{product_id}/ — High-res suit shots.

/categories/ — Icons and banners.

/users/{user_id}/measurements/ — Private customer data.

/reviews/{review_id}/ — Customer-uploaded photos.
